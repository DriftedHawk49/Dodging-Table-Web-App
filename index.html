How To Play

This game will train you with dodging tables. We all learn tables at our earlier classes, But we all tend to forget them, and with extensive use of calculators, we just avoid tables because we can do multiplication with calculator anyway.

This game consists of 4 modes, namely - Easy, Intermediate, Hard and God.

Easy
This mode is for Kids upto 5th Standard. It consists questions from multiplication tables of range 2 to 10. 10s are granted for every question, so there's time associated with this game (that is why it is called Dodging tables).
Intermediate
This mode is for kids upto 10th standard, It consists questions from multiplication tables of range 2 to 20. 7s are granted for every question.
Hard
This mode is for kids upto 12th standard, It consists questions from multiplication tables of range 12 to 25. 5s are granted for every question.
God
This mode is for people who are memory champions, or want to be.
It consists questions from multiplication tables of range 25 to 50. 5s are granted for every question.
Enter the Answer within time limit in the given space. While Entering the Digit, you don't have to press any key to submit your answer, it will automatically submit once timer is done.
